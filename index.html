✦ Everything you need

Built for the long story

Every feature in Yarn exists to help you write more, worry less, and leave something lasting behind.

✍️

Focused Writing Editor

A clean, simple, distraction-free editor built for stories. Type naturally — Yarn auto-tags the people, places, and topics you mention. Autosaves every few seconds.

🕰️

Timeline & Life Epochs

See your life chronologically. Threads are grouped into life epochs — Childhood, Young Adult, Middle Age, Later Years — so the shape of your story becomes clear.

💡

Thousands of Memory Prompts

One tap surfaces a curated question to spark a memory — from childhood and school days, to love, career turning points, and hard-won wisdom. Never face a blank page again.

👥

Editor Collaboration Subscription

Invite a family member or friend as an editor using a secure 8-character code. They can read your threads and ask questions to help fill the gaps — you stay in full control of what they see.

📸

Photos, Audio & Video

Attach media directly into your threads. Photos, voice recordings, video clips — each with their own captions and tags so nothing gets lost in a camera roll.

📖

Export Your Memoir

Export your story as a beautifully formatted HTML, EPUB, or PDF. Share by AirDrop, email, or save to Files. Your words, always in your hands — stored in open Markdown, never locked in.

🕰️ Timeline

Your whole life,
in one view.

Every thread you write, every milestone you record, every key date from your life — all laid out in chronological order. Tap any event to open it, filter by person or place, and export selected moments as a formatted document.

  • ✓ Pages, milestones, and key dates all unified
  • ✓ 50+ content-aware icons automatically matched
  • ✓ Colour-coded by life category
  • ✓ Filter by person, place, or status
  • ✓ Multi-select and export any slice of your story

👥 Editor Collaboration  Subscription only

You'll never write
alone.

Invite a family member or close friend as an editor using a secure 8-character code from Settings → My Editors. They can read your threads, ask questions to help you fill in the gaps, and encourage the journey — while you stay in full control of every word they can see. Available with an active subscription.

  • ✓ Invite editors with a secure 8-character code
  • ✓ Editors can send questions and reminders to inspire writing
  • ✓ Editors can view progress and offer encouragement
  • ✓ Editors can add questions and comments inside a thread
  • ✓ Editors can read and suggest edits — all changes saved
  • ✓ An editor can assist multiple authors
  • ✓ Private threads stay completely hidden
  • ✓ Revoke access any time, instantly
