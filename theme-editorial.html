Chapter II — Features

Built for the long story

Every feature in Yarn exists to help you write more, worry less, and leave something lasting behind.

01

Focused Writing Editor

A clean, distraction-free editor built for stories. Type naturally — Yarn auto-tags the people, places, and topics you mention. Autosaves every few seconds.

02

Timeline & Life Epochs

See your life chronologically. Threads group into life epochs — Childhood, Young Adult, Middle Age, Later Years — so the shape of your story becomes clear.

03

Thousands of Memory Prompts

One tap surfaces a curated question to spark a memory — from childhood and school days to love, career turning points, and hard-won wisdom.

04

Editor Collaboration

Invite a family member as an editor using a secure 8-character code. They can read your threads, ask questions to help fill the gaps, and encourage the journey.

05

Photos, Audio & Video

Attach photos, voice recordings, and video clips directly into your threads. Each with captions and tags so nothing gets lost in a camera roll.

06

Export Your Memoir

Export as HTML, EPUB, or PDF. Share by AirDrop, email, or save to Files. Stored in open Markdown — never locked in, always yours.
