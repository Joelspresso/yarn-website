✦ How it works

Everything you need to tell your story

Timeline

Your whole life, in one view.

Every thread you write and every milestone you record is laid out in chronological order — grouped into life epochs, colour-coded, and filterable by the people and places that mattered most.

Memory Prompts

Never face a blank page again.

Thousands of curated questions designed to unlock memories you didn't know you still had. A fresh prompt every day — covering childhood, love, career, travel, and everything in between.

Collaboration

You'll never write alone.

Invite family or friends as editors using a secure code. They can read your threads, prompt new memories, and encourage the journey — while you stay in full control of every word they see.
