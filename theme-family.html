✦ Why Yarn

Stories that outlive us all

Every family has stories that only one person remembers. The beach house summers. The first job nerves. The day everything changed. Yarn is built to help you capture them before they're gone.

Write naturally — Yarn organises everything for you. People, places, time periods, milestones. It all comes together into a memoir your family will treasure forever.

✦ Everything you need

Built for the long story

Every feature in Yarn exists to help you write more, worry less, and leave something lasting behind.

✍️

Focused Writing Editor

A clean, distraction-free editor built for stories. Type naturally — Yarn auto-tags the people, places, and topics you mention.

🕰️

Timeline & Life Epochs

See your life chronologically. Threads group into life epochs — Childhood, Young Adult, Middle Age, Later Years.

💡

Thousands of Memory Prompts

One tap surfaces a curated question to spark a memory — from childhood and school days to love, career, and hard-won wisdom.

👥

Editor Collaboration

Invite a family member as an editor using a secure code. They can read your threads, ask questions, and help fill the gaps.

📸

Photos, Audio & Video

Attach photos, voice recordings, and video clips directly into your threads. Each with captions and tags so nothing gets lost.

📖

Export Your Memoir

Export as HTML, EPUB, or PDF. Share by AirDrop, email, or save to Files. Stored in open Markdown — never locked in.

🕰️ Timeline

Every thread you write, every milestone you record, every key date — all laid out in chronological order. Tap any event to open it, filter by person or place, and export selected moments.
